Paragraphs and Line Breaks

You can write a paragraph in any way you want as long your content is inside the paragraph tag.
But keep in mind that your text will continue to be linear unless you use another tag to change it otherwise

You can break your paragraph using the tag "<br>" which I'll use to break the previous paragraph.

We can also add symbols html: © ® ™ € and many more.
